Max
Maxine "Max" Mayfield[4] is a major character of Stranger Things. She is portrayed by Sadie Sink. After her parents' divorce and her mother's remarriage, Max and her family moved from California to Hawkins, Indiana in October 1984. As the new student at Hawkins Middle School, she started forming a friendship with Lucas Sinclair, Dustin Henderson, Mike Wheeler, and Will Byers. Eventually, Max discovered the paranormal happenings in her new town. Max would go on to help her new friends burn the hub of the underground tunnel system, distracting the Demodogs so Eleven could close the Gate.
